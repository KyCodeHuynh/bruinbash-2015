How do I get in?

One “ticket” will be placed on every student’s BruinCard. In order to receive a wristband for the concert, come to the IM during the allotted time frame of 2:00pm- 6:00pm with your BruinCard. Your card will be scanned, and you will then have the opportunity to pick up a wristband on the IM field. No wristbands will be given if the student of a presented BruinCard is not physically present. Once you receive your wristband, go check out the sponsors on the field and score some sick giveaways! Line- up to enter Pauley will open at 5:00pm on the IM Field, and the doors will open at 6:00pm. No bags/purses/fanny packs/bottles/ etc. are permitted on the IM Field. All you will need is BruinCard and (optional) money for concessions, which will be sold in Pauley.

Can my friend use my BruinCard to pick up a wristband?

No, you must be present with your own BruinCard to get a wristband

What are the wristbands used for?

Wristbands picked up at the IM Field will be used for entrance to the concert. A separate wristbanding system will be used for the film (see below), and the Festival does not require a wristband for entry. In ALL cases, however, you will need to have a valid BruinCard to partake in these three events.

Will there be campouts this year?

There will be absolutely NO campouts this year. Any attempts at forming a lineup before set entry time to the IM Field (2:00pm-6:00pm) are prohibited and will result in immediate disbursement of the line by on-duty security. All students who are interested in attending the concert will be able to receive a wristband, and will be able to sit with their group of friends. Therefore, there can be no attempts at lining up for a wristband until 2:00pm.
