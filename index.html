CONCERT

8PM AT PAULEY PAVILLION

Hugo Leclercq aka Madeon has been called a genius and even a music prodigy. Hailing from Nantes, France, he began learning how to make music at age 12 and now is one of the world’s biggest music producers at age 21. His career skyrocketed with his mashup “Pop Culture” which combined forty pop songs in four minutes, and has received over 30 million views on YouTube. Ever since, Madeon has been working to produce songs with some of the biggest names out there, from Two Door Cinema Club’s single “Changing of the Seasons” to Lady Gaga’s most recent Artpop album to Coldplay’s latest release, Ghost Stories.

Recently, Madeon has been hitting the biggest fests, from EDC to Coachella, and touring to promote his most recent release. His newest album Adventure features vocals from some of the biggest indie artists out there, including Bastille, Passion Pit, Walk the Moon, and Foster the People. The album peaked at number one on the US Billboard EDM charts upon its release, and has been applauded by critics across the board, with Sputnikmusic praising Adventure to be “an album that needs to be in your upcoming summer playlist”.

Madeon has been taking the EDM world by storm and is now taking on his next stage, as our headliner for this year’s Bruin Bash.
