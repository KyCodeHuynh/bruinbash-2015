Hugo Leclercq aka Madeon has been called a genius and even a music prodigy. Hailing from Nantes, France, he began learning how to make music at age 11 and now, at age 21, is one of the world’s biggest music producers. His career skyrocketed with his youtube mashup “Pop Culture” which combined 39 pop songs in 3 and a half minutes, and has received over 30 million views on YouTube. Madeon has also produced and written for some of the biggest artists in the world such as Muse, Ellie Goulding, Coldplay, Two Door Cinema as well as Lady Gaga who he wrote and produced 3 songs for on her latest album ‘Artpop.'
Recently, Madeon has been appearing at some the biggest festivals in the world, from EDC to Coachella. He has also been touring to promote his most recent release —Adventure which features his vocals alongside guest features from some of the world's top artists; including Bastille, Passion Pit, Walk the Moon, and Foster the People . The album peaked at number one on the US Billboard Dance/Electronic chart upon its release, and has been applauded by critics around the world.

Madeon has been taking the EDM world by storm and is now taking on his next stage, as our headliner for this year’s Bruin Bash.

Anderson .Paak and producer Knxwledge are working together as NxWorries. They debuted with the single “ Suede” last spring.


Anderson .Paak is “ The L.A. Savant All Over Dr. Dre's New Album ” to quote the wise folks at Fader. The singer is featured on Dre’s Compton, but his own credits go beyond vocalist – songwriter, drummer, live performer and producer, whose music formed much of Watsky’s All You Can Do (2014).

Knxwledge is a prolific hip-hop beat maker from Los Angeles whose record Hud Dreems was released this year. His production credits include tracks for Kendrick Lamar, Joey Badass, Sandman, and a few other lucky collaborators.


NxWorries are working on a full-length album for OBE/Stones Throw Records, and are currently on tour with Earl Sweatshirt.

Prior to the opening act, ACA Hip Hop will be performing a tribute to Tracy Nguyen.
